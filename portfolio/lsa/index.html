Welcome to Little Star Adademy

Daycare, preschool, and kindergarten

Thank you for visiting Little Stars Academy Preschool in Arleta, California. The school provides toddler care, preschool and kindergarten in Arleta as well as the surrounding communities of San Fernando, Northridge, Van Nuys, North Hollywood, Granada Hills, Panoroma City, Lake View Terrace, Shadow Hills, North Hills and Mission Hills. This is a brand new school with the objective providing the best preschool experience for both the child and the parent.

Licensed through the California Department Of Social Services, we offer childcare in a loving environment for the preschool child age 18 months through 6 years old. The Center is open from 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM. Part time and full time programs are available. We operate year round and accept state paid programs such as CCRC and others. You are invited to come by and see the facility and meet the wonderful staff. You are welcom anytime although it is best to call ahead so creates a minimum of disruption to the school

Friendly Place

The Best Preschool in Arleta

Please come and take a tour and discover for yourself.

We believe that a good child care center must start with a clean and safe environnment. It must be visually attractive and provide good equipment and learning materials. The teachers have to be selected not only for their education and knowledge but also because they genuinely understand and love children. Your child should have the opportunity to enjoy good quality food and have access to high quality teaching materials. We believe we are well on that way to create that perfect place for your child.
